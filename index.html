FRIED CHICKEN

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35mins

Additional Time: 30mins

Total Time: 1hr 20mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

How to Make Crispy Fried Chicken

You'll find the step-by-step recipe below, but here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this crispy fried chicken:

  1. Step 1

    2. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer.

  2. Step 2

    3. Put the flour in a large plastic bag (let the amount of chicken you are cooking dictate the amount of flour you use). Season the flour with paprika, salt and
    pepper to taste (paprika helps to brown the chicken).

  3. Step 3

    4. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.

  4. step 4

    5. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY.
    THIS IS CRUCIAL!

  5. Step 5

    6. Fill a large skillet (cast iron is best) about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil. Heat until VERY hot.

  6. Step 6

    7. Put in as many chicken pieces as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in HOT oil on both sides.

  7. Step 7

    8. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy). Remove cover, raise heat again,
    and continue to fry until crispy.

  8. Step 8

    9. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels. Depending on how much chicken you have, you may have to fry in a few shifts. Keep the finished chicken in a slightly
    warm oven while preparing the rest.
